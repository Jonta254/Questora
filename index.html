1
Connect your Pi account

Open Questora inside Pi Browser. Tap π Connect on the Home page. Your Pioneer username links to your progress.

2
Complete today's daily quest

Every day a new question appears. Answer correctly to earn points and extend your streak. One quest per day — come back tomorrow for the next.

3
Learn through categories

Open Learn to choose a category. Each has short lessons, a question, and a reward. Answer correctly to unlock the badge and earn points.

4
Explore the Pioneer Hub

The π Pioneer tab teaches you about Pi Network — phases, mining, KYC, wallet safety, and ranks. Answer the daily Pi quiz for extra points.

5
Track rewards and unlock premium

Go to 🏆 Rewards to see your tier progress. When you have earned enough, optional premium packs deepen your learning with real Pi payments.

6
Earn achievements

Badges unlock automatically as you learn. Reach 300 pts to become a Pioneer, 800 pts for Miner, and 3500 pts for Ambassador.

🤝
Respect every Pioneer

Questora is a global app used by Pioneers of all ages. Be kind, supportive, and inclusive in referrals and interactions.

🎓
Learn honestly

Answer questions using your own knowledge. The goal is growth — guessing through everything defeats the purpose and reduces your ranking quality.

🔒
Keep Pi safe

Never share your Pi wallet passphrase with anyone — not even if someone claims to be a Questora admin. No legitimate app or team will ask for it.

🌍
Represent your country fairly

Your region selection affects leaderboard and content. Select your actual country so the global Pioneer community stays accurate.

Pi Payments

Pi payments are only processed inside Pi Browser. Questora will never ask for payment outside the official Pi payment flow. Payments are voluntary and optional — all free learning remains available.

Daily Limits

Each daily quest can only be completed once per day. Daily booster points reset at midnight UTC. This keeps learning streaks meaningful and prevents point farming.

Reward Records

Payout review records are in-app point logs only. They are not real Pi transfers. Questora clearly separates app points from Pi blockchain transactions.

Age & Content

Questora is designed for all ages. Some premium packs contain adult financial and creator topics. Parents can keep the app set to the Kids or Teens age group to filter appropriately.

Account Reset

Resetting your progress is permanent and cannot be undone. All points, streaks, lessons, and achievement badges will be cleared from this device.

Share Questora with Pioneers in your Security Circle. Each friend who joins and completes their first quest earns you bonus points.
